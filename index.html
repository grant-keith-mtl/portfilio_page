A problem solver with a knack for creation

A New York native, I came to Montreal, Canada to attend McGill University studying Information Systems in the Desautels Faculty of Management in the Fall of 2016. During my 3rd and 4th years, I became very interested in problem solving, via software. I have fallen in love with breaking down problems, and then coming up with novel ways of implementing solutions. That feeling of spending hours iterating through different ideas and use cases, to finally reach that sense of accomplishment upon completion, only to move onto the next problem, really motivates me to keep on keeping on and continuously learn and expand upon my skillsets. After taking a course on HTML, CSS, and Javascript, I proceeded to continue taking other software courses, including courses on Discrete Structures, C, Python for Data Science, Java, Discrete Structures, and Algorithm Design.
‍
Outside of programming, I enjoy activites such as playing soccer, going to the gym, playing video games, and attempting to learn to play the guitar (somewhat unsuccessfully). I also really enjoy travelling and am working on slowly cutting down my bucket list of places to visit.
